About

OMOS started as a local draft.

Originally, "Open Minds Open Source" was architected as a podcast dedicated to documenting the stories of Indian developers behind the world's most critical open source infrastructure. It was a tribute to the architects of the code we all rely on—but that project remained in staging, never successfully deploying to production.

We took that unmerged branch and refactored the mission.

We realized the open source community needed more than just a playback device; it needed a dedicated operating system. Today, OMOS has evolved from an unlaunched concept into a comprehensive Open Source Hub.

We are building the interface for the community:

The Directory: A curated registry of high-impact projects and the maintainers who ship them.

The Blog: Editorial documentation, deep-dives, and community narratives.

The Podcast: Finally executing the original script—conversations with the pioneers and creators shaping the open source landscape.

OMOS.dev is now live. Access granted.
